This website is about the three ships of the three big allied nations who has served in the war.

World War 2 was the largest conflict that occurred in 20th century that put multiple countries and nations against one another. It was also the most destructive war in history, with the invention of multiple military advancements and technology such as nuclear weaponry and the advancement in naval warfare, the war has impacted everyone and every nation in the world. In the naval side, WW2 was the next step of naval warfare, implementing strategies and creating ships of terrifying capabilities. These ships were powerful forces that helped advance their war efforts. with multiple fleets at the hands of commanders, they lead the theatres to win the battles towards their victory.

World War 2 was fought between two groups of nations - The Allies consisting of United States, United Kingdom and the Soviet Union; and The Axis - consisting of the German Reich, Kingdom of Italy and the Empire of Japan. Note that they are not the only ones participating in the conflict, as there are many more who has teamed up with either side. Both parties boasted military prowess, and naval technologies that were innovative yet deadly and terrifying. Submarines, aircraft carriers, landing crafts and radar and sonar techology were only a few features of the naval innovations.
